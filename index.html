Why Convert JPG to PNG?

PNG format supports transparency (alpha channel) and uses lossless compression, which means your image keeps its full quality. This makes PNG the better choice for logos, icons, graphics, screenshots, UI designs, and images with text or sharp edges. JPG works great for photos, but if you need pixel-perfect quality or a transparent background, PNG is the way to go.

The JPG to PNG conversion preserves the full visual quality of your original image. Since PNG uses lossless compression, what you see is exactly what you get. File size may increase slightly compared to JPG, but you get a cleaner image with no compression artifacts.
